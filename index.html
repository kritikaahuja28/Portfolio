About Me

I am a Senior AI Developer at Ernst & Young (EY), with a strong passion for leveraging artificial intelligence to solve complex business challenges. Based in Chandigarh, India, I specialize in developing and implementing advanced AI and machine learning solutions, particularly in the areas of automation, natural language processing, and computer vision. My expertise spans across Python, machine learning algorithms, and frameworks such as OpenCV, Flask, and currently working in FastAPI.

With a dual degree in BE-MBA in Computer Science Engineering from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, I have developed a deep understanding of both technical and business aspects of AI solutions. Over the years, I have contributed to a wide range of projects, from developing Gen-AI solutions to integrating OpenAI APIs and automating document generation processes. I am passionate about driving innovation and improving operational efficiency through AI.

In addition to my professional work, I have been recognized for my contributions, including winning multiple hackathons such as the Smart India Hackathon 2020 and Bosch AI Hackathon 2021. I am also actively involved in extracurricular activities, including volunteering for community events like blood donation camps.

I am always eager to explore new AI trends and look forward to contributing to impactful projects that drive positive change.
